Dante Romita: An Autobiography

Introduction:

At the time of my writing this, I am a 19-Year-Old Canadian with an Italian background. My pronouns are he/him. I'm also currently a second-year university student majoring in Computer Science.

My Mission:

My mission has been and always will be to spread kindness, positivity, love and support to everyone. I also advocate for justice and morality every day, striving for a better tomorrow with an esteemed sense of optimism. As we all know, the internet contains too much negativity, hatred and toxicity. Once I found my calling, I realized just how devastating an impact these influences can have on us. My conscience knows that I shouldn't tolerate this as the norm. Everyone who seeks love and acceptance deserves to be treated as such. That's why I began becoming a positive online influencer. I will commit to carrying forth this mission and support as many people as I can until the day I die.
